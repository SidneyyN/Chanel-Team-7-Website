User Manual

How to Access App in Browser

1. Log in to PowerApps using the provided login details*.

The app is only accessible via a separate Microsoft organization we created as a team. COMP0016 markers can find the login details in their UCL emails. After logging in, please head to ‘PowerApps’
* Please contact Ashley (cheuk.tam.22@ucl.ac.uk) if there are any issues

2. Make sure you are in the default MSFT environment. If not, click the ‘environment’ button and pick MSFT (default).

3. Click the Chanel Post-Sales Management App .

How to use PowerApps in Browser

1. Click through screens and components on the panel to the left to find the code in our app.

Most code is embedded into individual components. Pick a component on the panel to inspect it, then read the text box on top, or ‘advanced’ menu on the right to review code.

2. Click the play button on the top-right to preview the app.

Interact with the app as you would on your mobile device

How to Access App on Mobile

1. Install PowerApps on your mobile device through the app store

2. Log into the same user_test Microsoft account

3. Click onto Chanel Post-Sales Management App

4. Use the app as the end-user would

Contract Agreement

Our product has been meticulously developed as a closed-source software, ensuring that its source code remains proprietary and exclusive to our organization. This strategic decision is instrumental in safeguarding our innovative solutions and maintaining our competitive advantage in the market. It is important to note that our application does not collect, store, or process any public or customer data, thereby significantly minimizing privacy concerns and compliance requirements associated with data protection regulations. We have implemented stringent internal protocols to ensure the integrity and security of our software, aligning with best practices in software development and intellectual property protection. For any inquiries regarding the legal aspects, usage, or processes surrounding our product, we encourage direct communication with Chanel. Chanel's legal team is well-equipped to address any questions or concerns, providing clear and comprehensive information to ensure transparency and trust with our users and stakeholders.
