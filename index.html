POSTSALES MOBILE APP FOR CHANEL

COMP0016 Team 7

Abstract

Chanel is a French luxury fashion house. In this project, we worked with their Fashion department to deliver an internal mobile application that provides real-time visibilty to after-sales items and streamline the entire process of exportation in the London flagship boutique.

With this project, the mobile app would be able to replace the archaic paper tracking of items, and enable employees to increase their efficiency and reduce errors, ultimately improving customer satisfaction.
