Abstract

Chanel stands as an iconic French luxury fashion house, renowned for its unparalleled elegance and style. In this venture, we had the privilege of collaborating closely with Chanel's Fashion department. Our objective was to create an internal mobile application designed to revolutionize the way after-sales items are managed, offering real-time visibility and significantly streamlining the exportation process within their flagship boutique in London.

This project aimed to transcend traditional methodologies by replacing outdated paper-based tracking systems with a cutting-edge mobile application. By embracing this digital transformation, Chanel's employees are now empowered to enhance their operational efficiency and minimize the likelihood of errors. Such advancements not only optimize internal workflows but also elevate the overall customer experience, ensuring satisfaction remains at the heart of Chanel's after-sales service. This innovative approach marks a significant milestone in Chanel's journey towards integrating technology with luxury retail, setting new standards for excellence in customer service and operational efficiency.

Features

Have a look at the features of our app.

01

Barcode Scanning

Our app will provide a barcode scanner, where Fashion Advisors can scan barcodes attached on drawers to access order details. This removes the need of paper forms for each order, which can further improve time efficiency.

02

Order Editing

Fashion Advisors can then edit the details and status of each orders on the app, without wasting time fumbling around to find the corresponding paper forms. Fashion advisors can also include pictures of receipts in the order details.

03

Shipment and Delivery

When an order is ready, Fashion Advisors can send it off and place the order in the Shipped Category. Links for couriers can be seen here, where fashion advisors can click into and track the location of the order.
